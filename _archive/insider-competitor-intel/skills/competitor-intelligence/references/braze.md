# Braze — Competitor Reference

## Overview
Braze is a leading customer engagement platform focused on real-time, cross-channel messaging for consumer-facing brands. Headquartered in New York, publicly traded (BRZE). Primarily targets mid-market to enterprise B2C companies in retail, media, gaming, fintech, and travel.

## Positioning
"Braze is a leading customer engagement platform that powers lasting connections between consumers and brands they love." Positions as the best-in-class engagement layer — assumes you have a CDP elsewhere. Heavy emphasis on real-time triggers and personalization at scale.

## Core Product Areas

### Channels
- Email, SMS/MMS, Push (Web + Mobile), In-App Messages, Content Cards, News Feed, WhatsApp, LINE, Webhooks, Connected Content

### Segmentation
- Segment Extensions (SQL-based and no-code), behavioral filters, custom attributes, predictive churn/purchase (Predictive Suite), real-time and batch

### Journey Orchestration
- Canvas Flow: visual multi-step, multi-channel journey builder with branching, delay steps, action-based triggers, experiment steps (A/B/multivariate)

### Personalization & AI
- Liquid templating for dynamic content, Connected Content (live API calls at send time), Sage AI (Braze's AI suite): intelligent timing, intelligent channel, predictive churn, predictive purchase, AI copy assistant, feature flags for experimentation

### Data & CDP
- Not a native CDP — relies on SDKs and integrations to ingest data. Cloud Data Ingestion (CDI) syncs from Snowflake, Databricks, BigQuery, Redshift. Currents for real-time data export to data warehouses.

### Analytics
- Campaign Analytics, Canvas Analytics, Retention Reports, Funnel Reports, Revenue Reports, Global Control Group, A/B testing

### Developer Experience
- REST API, SDKs (iOS, Android, Web, React Native, Flutter, Unity, Cordova, Xamarin, Roku), Webhooks, Braze-to-Braze events, Postman collection

### Integrations
- 150+ technology partners (Amplitude, mParticle, Segment, Snowflake, Salesforce, etc.), Alloys partner program

## Strengths
- Deep channel breadth and real-time triggering
- Strong developer ecosystem and SDK quality
- Sage AI suite gaining traction
- Excellent documentation and community
- Strong enterprise compliance (SOC2, HIPAA, GDPR)

## Weaknesses
- Not a native CDP — data model is flat without a warehouse
- Pricing can be expensive at scale (MAU + message volume)
- Analytics are engagement-centric, not revenue-centric
- Limited e-commerce / product catalog features out of the box
- No native web personalization layer

## Pricing Model
MAU-based + message volume add-ons. Enterprise contracts. Not publicly listed.

## Key Differentiators vs. Insider One
- Braze has deeper WhatsApp/LINE support in APAC
- Insider One has stronger onsite/web personalization and product recommendations
- Insider One's CDP is more native; Braze relies on integrations for unified profile
- Braze has broader partner ecosystem
- Insider One often wins on AI-driven product discovery in e-commerce verticals

## Recommended Talking Points (Insider vs. Braze)
- "Insider includes a built-in CDP — you don't need to buy and stitch a separate data layer"
- "Insider's onsite personalization covers the full customer journey, not just messaging channels"
- "Insider's AI-powered product recommendations drive directly measurable revenue lift, not just engagement metrics"
- "Insider's pricing is more predictable for high-volume e-commerce"
