# MoEngage — Competitor Reference

## Overview
MoEngage is a mobile-first customer engagement platform founded in 2014, headquartered in San Francisco. Strong presence in Asia-Pacific, South Asia, Middle East, and Africa. Targets mid-market and enterprise B2C brands across retail, banking/fintech, media, and travel.

## Positioning
"MoEngage is an insights-led engagement platform trusted by 1,200+ global consumer brands." Focuses on AI-powered analytics and personalization to drive retention. Strong mobile-first narrative.

## Core Product Areas

### Channels
- Push Notifications (Mobile + Web), In-App Messages, Email, SMS, WhatsApp, On-site Notifications, Cards/Feed, Connectors (webhook-based integrations)

### Segmentation
- RFM analysis, behavioral cohorts, predictive segments (powered by Sherpa AI), funnel-based segmentation, live vs. batch

### Journey Orchestration
- Flows: drag-and-drop multi-channel journey builder with branching, frequency capping, A/B testing within flows

### Personalization & AI
- Sherpa AI: send time optimization, channel optimization, predictive segments, product recommendations, next best action. Dynamic content personalization in messages.

### Data & CDP
- Customer 360 profile, unified customer timeline. Ingestion via SDKs, REST API, data import. Limited native CDP — primarily aggregates behavioral data. Integrates with external CDPs (Segment, mParticle, RudderStack).

### Analytics
- Funnel analysis, retention cohorts, uninstall tracking, revenue analytics, campaign analytics, Custom Dashboards

### Developer Experience
- SDKs: iOS, Android, Web, React Native, Flutter, Cordova, Unity, Xamarin, Capacitor, Ionic, Roku. REST API for events, users, campaigns.

### Integrations
- 100+ integrations: data platforms (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift), analytics (Mixpanel, Amplitude), CRMs, payment gateways, CDPs

## Strengths
- Strong mobile SDK ecosystem
- Deep APAC/MENA market penetration and localization
- Sherpa AI is well-regarded for predictive segmentation
- Competitive pricing for mid-market
- Strong WhatsApp Business API integration

## Weaknesses
- Weaker onsite/web personalization compared to Insider
- CDP functionality is less mature than pure-play CDPs
- Email deliverability and template builder less sophisticated than competitors
- Less brand recognition in North America/Europe vs. Braze or Klaviyo
- Enterprise readiness (compliance certifications) still catching up

## Pricing Model
Contact-based (Monthly Active Users). Modular pricing — channels can be purchased separately. Mid-market friendly.

## Key Differentiators vs. Insider One
- MoEngage is stronger in Android-heavy mobile markets
- Insider One has more robust web/onsite capabilities
- Both compete heavily in APAC and MENA — direct head-to-head accounts common
- Insider One's CDP is more mature; MoEngage is more messaging-layer focused
- Insider One has stronger e-commerce product catalog and recommendation engine

## Recommended Talking Points (Insider vs. MoEngage)
- "Insider's web personalization and A/B testing capabilities go well beyond mobile notifications"
- "Insider's AI recommendations are deeply integrated with product catalog data — not just behavioral signals"
- "Insider's unified CDP means fewer integration touch points across web, app, and messaging"
- "Insider's reporting connects engagement to revenue, not just clicks and opens"
